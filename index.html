About the Academy

The Malawi Academy for Sports provides young athletes with training, guidance, and opportunities to develop their talent across various sports. Our mission is to nurture disciplined, skilled, and competitive athletes who can represent Malawi nationally and internationally.

Our Mission

To nurture disciplined, skilled, and competitive athletes, including those with disabilities, in an inclusive environment where everyone can represent Malawi nationally and internationally.

Our Vision

To become Malawi's leading sports academy recognized for excellence, professionalism, inclusion, and talent development.
