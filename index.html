About the Academy

The Malawi Academy for Sports provides young athletes with professional training, guidance, and opportunities to develop their talents across various sports. Its mission is to nurture disciplined, skilled, and competitive athletes capable of representing Malawi at both national and international levels.

The Malawi Academy for Sports is an initiative established by the Malawi National Council of Sports to promote the development of sports in the country, particularly at the grassroots level. The Academy targets youth under the age of 18.

Currently, the program operates in three centers: Blantyre Youth Centre in the Southern Region, Kamuzu Institute for Sport in the Central Region, and Katoto Ground in the Northern Region. More than 600 young athletes aged between six and 18 are actively participating in the program.

Our Mission

To nurture disciplined, skilled, and competitive athletes, including those with disabilities, in an inclusive environment where everyone can represent Malawi nationally and internationally.

Our Vision

To become Malawi's leading sports academy recognized for excellence, professionalism, inclusion, and talent development.
